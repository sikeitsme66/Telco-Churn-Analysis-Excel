# 📊 Telecom Churn Analysis (Excel Project)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an end-to-end churn analysis of a telecom company using advanced Excel tools. The goal was to identify key drivers of customer churn, quantify revenue risk, and build an interactive dashboard for management to make data-driven retention decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Calculate overall and segment-wise churn rates
- Identify causes of churn (Contract Type, Internet Service, Payment Method, Number of Services)
- Analyze service stickiness and customer loyalty
- Quantify revenue at risk due to churn
- Build an interactive dashboard to track churn KPIs and provide actionable insights

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Skills Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query (ETL)
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Dashboard Design

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Telecom customer dataset (CSV format)
- Contains customer info, contract details, internet service, payment method, and service usage

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- Interactive filters 
- KPI Cards:
  - Churn Rate
  - Monthly Revenue Leak
  - Revenue at High Risk
- Clear visual insights for decision making and management

---

## 💡 Key Insights
- Month-to-month contracts drive most churn
- Higher-paying users are more likely to leave
- Fiber optic and electronic check customers are high-risk
- Customers with multiple services are more loyal
- Early months are crucial for retention efforts

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations
- Implement retention programs for first-year customers
- Offer loyalty discounts to switch from month-to-month to 1-year contracts
- Provide bundled services including tech support and online security
- Incentivize auto-pay to reduce churn from payment issues
- Target high-risk segments (fiber users, electronic check users) with proactive support
